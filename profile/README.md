## How it works

1. Describe a decision situation in plain text
2. Lagbase generates a Decision Lag Artifact (DLA)
3. Share the artifact â€” it's a mirror with timestamps

DLAs surface what's already true: claims vs evidence, who decides vs who delays, what was known and when. No recommendations. No automation. Just clarity.

## Decision Lag Artifacts

Portable, neutral, timestamp-verified objects that turn organizational fog into legible decision surfaces.

| Artifact | What It Surfaces |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Reality vs Narrative** | Gaps between claims and evidence |
| **Decision Surface Map** | Who decides, what blocks, reversibility |
| **Time-Since-Known** | How long issues have been known without action |
| **Accountability Topology** | Who can decide, who pays, who delays |
| **Drift & Delta Ledger** | Gap between original intent and current reality |
| **Evidence Chain Map** | Where claim chains break |
